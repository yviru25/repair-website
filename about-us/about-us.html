How we started
The founder of iFix DMV has always had a special passion for electronics and technology since childhood. As an IT professional, his family and friends always contacted him for help when they have a broken cell phone screen, computer issues and problem with other tech gadgets. He has been fixing cell phones, computer and other tech gadgets for over 10 years.
Based in the Washington, DC area, our founder realized that it is not always easy to find reliable tech repair services when and where you need them the most; especially at this moment in time when our mobile devices and tech gadgets are so valuable to us that they are now part of our daily work and personal lives. With that in mind, iFix DMV was started to initially serve the DC, MD and VA area usually referred to as DMV area.
